# Proposal: cut inbound ticket volume fast (Priya, PM)

**Goal:** get the weekly ticket number down before the board review.

**Plan:**
1. Add an **AI chatbot** (vendor: DeflectAI) on the support page. Their demo shows
   it auto-answers common questions and **deflects ~35% of inbound tickets**.
   Quote: $2,000/mo, live in **2 weeks**.
2. Add an **FAQ interstitial**: before a user can reach the "Contact Support" form,
   they must scroll through an FAQ page and click "my issue isn't here."

**Why:** this is the fastest lever. Combined, we estimate a 30-40% drop in logged
tickets within the first full week of launch. It directly moves the metric the
board is watching, and it needs no engineering from the product team.

**Risks:** some users may find the extra step annoying. Manageable.
