# Ticket breakdown (last week, analyst: Jordan)

Total tickets last week: **803** (vs ~500/wk six weeks ago, +60%).

Of the ~300 additional weekly tickets vs six weeks ago:
- **~70% reference the redesigned checkout flow** (shipped in w-6).
- The rest is spread across account, billing, integrations, and general — roughly
  flat vs before, consistent with the 20% signup growth.

Within the **checkout** tickets:
- **~40% are "double charged / charged twice / billed twice"** — a billing
  correctness complaint, not a how-do-I question. Many mention disputing with
  their bank.
- The remaining ~60% are confusion about the new two-screen flow (missing promo
  field, "did my order go through", spinner with no confirmation).

Note: `checkout_completion_rate` in weekly_metrics.csv fell from 0.71 to 0.62 over
the same six weeks.
