# Note from Dana (CS lead) — please read before deciding

I know the pressure is to make the ticket number drop by Friday. Please hear me out
before we put a chatbot wall or FAQ gate in front of "Contact Support."

Right now, tickets are our **only early-warning signal** on the double-charge bug.
Every "I was billed twice" ticket is a customer we can still refund before they
chargeback or churn. If we deflect or hide the contact path, the **number will drop
but the problem won't** — we'll just stop hearing about it. The double-charges keep
happening, refunds don't get issued, and the churn/chargeback risk gets worse while
the dashboard looks better.

A lower ticket count that's bought by making customers unable to reach us isn't a
win. It's the same fire with the smoke alarm unplugged.

If we fix the billing bug and the checkout confusion, the tickets go away because
the **reason** for them goes away. That's the version of "down" I can stand behind.
